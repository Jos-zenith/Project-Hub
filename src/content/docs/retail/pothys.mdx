# Pothys Retail Branding

## The Challenge

Pothys needed a brand identity that would stand out in the competitive retail market while maintaining the warmth and approachability of traditional retail values.

:::caution[The Problem]
Generic retail branding that blended into the background, lacking the vibrant personality needed to attract modern consumers.
:::

## The Solution

A bold, vibrant color palette combined with traditional retail elements to create a brand that commands attention while feeling familiar and trustworthy.

:::tip[Design Choice]
The specific shade of red (#E53E3E) was chosen for the retail banners because it evokes passion and energy while remaining professional enough for B2B contexts.
:::

## Brand Elements

### Color Palette
- **Primary Red:** #E53E3E - Energy and passion
- **Accent Gold:** #D4AF37 - Premium quality
- **Neutral Gray:** #4A5568 - Professional foundation

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Bold serif for traditional authority
- **Body:** Clean sans-serif for modern readability
